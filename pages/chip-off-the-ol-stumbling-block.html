Chip Off The Ol' Stumbling Block

A hand-crafted runner released on Steam

Game Steam Release 2022

Chip Off The Ol' Stumbling Block was borne of my own frustration of constantly starting and abandoning prototype projects, and represents my shift to dedicatedly finishing every project I start. It was my first release to Unity, and is based on a tutorial by the world-loved Brackeys.

Produced from a basic starter tutorial, I started with a list of five environments, 9 obstacle types, and a release date of fall 2022. It was a crash-course education on controlling scope, maintaining deadlines, and working with limited knowledge and resources to create the best product possible.
