Boston Game Dev

I help run events for this 501(c)(3) non-profit organization

Community Project Management Ongoing

I joined Boston Game Dev in 2021, attending online events and their annual cookout, but showed up with a camera when they mentioned they didn't have anyone to record. I became a valued member of the community, which eventually lead me to join the organizers, where I helped plan events. I started showing up early to help set up, cooking my World Famous macaroni salad for the barbeque, and even started running my own events!

From this experience, I gained a crash-course in event management, handling sudden changes (our first date was snowed out) and organizing groups of people to get things done!
