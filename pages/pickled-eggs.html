Pickled Eggs

A perfect Summer treat, a fun side-hustle, and an awesome conversation starter!

Side Hustle Food 2016-current

Pickled eggs are hard-boiled and peeled eggs that have been cured in vinegar and brine. They're often mixed with onion, pepper, garlic, and herbs and spices.

A long-time personal favorite snack and salad additive! I've been making pickled eggs all my adult life, and share them with anyone who's adventurous enough to try them! I sell them at yard sales $12 for a quart, $5 for a pint!
